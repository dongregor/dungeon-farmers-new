## üìä Leaderboards (Optional)

### Player Power Rankings
- Top 100 players by total Power
- Weekly rewards for Top 10/50/100
- Purely cosmetic titles, no power advantages

### Alliance Rankings
- Top 50 alliances by Alliance Level and contributions
- Seasonal exclusive cosmetics
- Bragging rights only

### Speedrun Leaderboards
- Fastest zone clears (per zone)
- Fastest dungeon clears (per dungeon)
- Fastest raid clears (per raid)
- Exclusive "Speedrunner" titles

**Design Rationale:** Competition is OPTIONAL. Casual players ignore leaderboards, competitive players chase rankings.

## üéØ Design Principles Applied

### Collection Focus (Not Grinding)
- Clear completion goals (150 monsters, 50 schematics)
- Milestones provide regular rewards
- Ultimate goal: "Ultimate Guild Master" title

### Optional Competition
- Leaderboards available for competitive players
- Casual players ignore them completely
- No power advantages from rankings

### Seasonal Content Rotation
- Quarterly events keep content fresh
- Events return annually (no FOMO)
- Always something new to look forward to

### Respect for Time
- Daily challenges: 10-15 minutes
- Weekly challenges: Natural through normal play
- No mandatory daily grinds

---

**Endgame Content provides months of meaningful progression through collection completion, build optimization, and optional competitive content - all without forcing endless grinding or FOMO mechanics.**
