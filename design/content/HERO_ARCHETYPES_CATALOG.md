# Hero Archetypes Catalog - Complete Class Guide

**Purpose**: Comprehensive guide to all 6 hero archetypes with stats, playstyles, and progression

**Philosophy**: Each archetype has distinct role, viable builds, and parody personality

---

## Archetype System Overview

### The 6 Archetypes
1. **Tank** - Defensive wall, protects allies
2. **Healer** - Support specialist, sustains team
3. **Ranged DPS** - Damage from distance, high crit
4. **Melee DPS** - Close combat damage dealer
5. **Caster** - Magic damage, AOE specialist
6. **Support** - Utility, buffs, debuffs

### Core Mechanics
- **Base Stats**: HP, ATK, DEF, SPD (scale with level 1-50)
- **Stat Multipliers**: Each archetype has unique scaling
- **Traits**: 3-5 traits modify base effectiveness
- **Equipment**: 6 slots, 30-40% of total power
- **Power Rating**: Total combat effectiveness score

## ARCHETYPE #1: TANK

### Core Identity
**Role**: Frontline defender, damage sponge, team protector

**Playstyle**: "I'm not stuck in here with you. You're stuck in here with me (and my high HP pool)."

**Motto**: *"They shall not pass (without filling out proper forms first)."*

### Base Stats (Level 1)
- **HP**: 250 (Highest)
- **ATK**: 40 (Second lowest)
- **DEF**: 50 (Highest)
- **SPD**: 30 (Lowest)

### Stat Scaling per Level
- **HP**: +15 per level (reaches 985 at level 50)
- **ATK**: +2.5 per level (reaches 162.5 at level 50)
- **DEF**: +3 per level (reaches 197 at level 50)
- **SPD**: +1 per level (reaches 79 at level 50)

### Power Rating Formula
```
Power = (HP × 0.4) + (ATK × 1.0) + (DEF × 1.5) + (SPD × 0.8) + Equipment Score
```

### Optimal Equipment Sets
1. **Guardian's Oath** (Rare) - Maximum HP/DEF bonuses
2. **Corporate Casual** (Uncommon) - Balanced starter
3. **Unkillable Build** (Mixed) - Extreme survivability focus

### Recommended Traits
- **Unkillable Cockroach** (Rare) - +30-40% HP, +15-20% DEF
- **Unbreakable Will** (Epic) - +40-55% HP, immune to morale debuffs
- **Team Player** (Common) - +3-6% All Stats with allies
- **Optimist** (Common) - +3-5% HP

### Parody Personality Archetypes
- **The Immovable Object**: "You hit me. I'm still here. Try again (I have all day)."
- **The Paperwork Fortress**: "You can't hurt me. I'm protected by 47 layers of bureaucracy."
- **The Meeting Attendee**: "I've sat through 8-hour meetings. Your attacks mean nothing."
- **OSHA Compliant**: "I'm wearing all required protective gear (it's uncomfortable but effective)."

### Strengths
- ✅ Highest survivability
- ✅ Best defensive stats
- ✅ Protects squishy allies
- ✅ Never dies in tutorial zones (embarrassing for everyone else)

### Weaknesses
- ❌ Lowest damage output
- ❌ Slowest speed
- ❌ Can't solo content efficiently
- ❌ Expeditions take longer

### Leveling Curve
- **Early Game (1-15)**: Dominant (unkillable in beginner zones)
- **Mid Game (16-35)**: Solid (needs damage support)
- **Late Game (36-50)**: Essential (endgame requires tanks)

### Playstyle Tips
- Pair with damage dealers (you're the shield, they're the sword)
- Focus on HP/DEF equipment
- Traits that boost survivability >> damage traits
- Expeditions: Slower but safer (60% efficiency minimum)

- **The Passive Aggressive Healer**: "I'll heal you (but I won't be happy about it)."

- ✅ Team survivability multiplier

### Weaknesses
- ❌ Lowest personal damage
- ❌ Relies on team for damage
- ❌ Solo content is painful
- ❌ Gets blamed anyway (always)

### Leveling Curve
- **Early Game (1-15)**: Unnecessary (enemies too weak)
- **Mid Game (16-35)**: Helpful (content gets harder)
- **Late Game (36-50)**: Mandatory (you will die without them)

### Playstyle Tips
- Focus on team compositions (never solo)
- Speed >>> Damage (faster heals save lives)
- Traits that boost team >> personal damage
- Expeditions: Team efficiency boost (+10-20%)

## ARCHETYPE #3: RANGED DPS

### Core Identity
**Role**: Damage from safety, crit specialist, glass cannon

**Playstyle**: "I hit hard from far away. Come closer? (No thank you.)"

**Motto**: *"The best offense is staying out of melee range."*

### Base Stats (Level 1)
- **HP**: 150
- **ATK**: 65 (Second highest)
- **DEF**: 30
- **SPD**: 50

### Stat Scaling per Level
- **HP**: +8 per level (reaches 542 at level 50)
- **ATK**: +4 per level (reaches 261 at level 50)
- **DEF**: +1.5 per level (reaches 103.5 at level 50)
- **SPD**: +3 per level (reaches 197 at level 50)

### Power Rating Formula
```
Power = (HP × 0.3) + (ATK × 2.0) + (DEF × 0.5) + (SPD × 1.5) + Equipment Score + (Crit × 1.5)
```

### Optimal Equipment Sets
1. **Shadow Stalker** (Epic) - Maximum crit and attack
2. **Grindstone Warrior** (Rare) - Farming efficiency
3. **Volcanic Fury** (Epic) - Elemental damage focus

### Recommended Traits
- **Glass Cannon** (Rare) - +25-35% ATK, -15-20% HP
- **Speedrunner** (Rare) - -15-25% duration, speed focus
- **Lucky** (Uncommon) - +5-8% crit chance
- **Sarcastic** (Uncommon) - +6-10% ATK (cutting remarks)

### Parody Personality Archetypes
- **The Sniper**: "I kill from 300 meters. Personal space: enforced."
- **The Glass Cannon**: "I dish out pain. I also die to strong breezes."
- **The Crit Fisher**: "99% of my damage is from crits. The other 1%? Also crits."
- **Social Distancing Expert**: "6 feet minimum. Preferably 600."

### Strengths
- ✅ Highest single-target damage
- ✅ Best crit potential
- ✅ Safe positioning (ranged)
- ✅ Speedrun meta (clear fast)

### Weaknesses
- ❌ Extremely fragile
- ❌ Dies if looked at wrong
- ❌ Requires positioning awareness
- ❌ Tank required for hard content

### Leveling Curve
- **Early Game (1-15)**: Overpowered (one-shot everything)
- **Mid Game (16-35)**: Strong (need defense support)
- **Late Game (36-50)**: Glass cannon (high risk high reward)

### Playstyle Tips
- Pair with tanks (they get hit, you don't)
- Focus on ATK/Crit equipment
- Speed > Survivability (kill before they reach you)
- Expeditions: High damage efficiency (+20-40%)

- **The Magic Minmaxer**: "I've optimized my build to 0.3% efficiency (worth it)."

## Design Philosophy

### Role Clarity
- Each archetype has distinct identity
- No "strictly better" archetype (all viable)
- Team composition matters
- Solo viability varies (intentional)

### Balance Principles
- **Rock-Paper-Scissors** avoidance (no hard counters)
- **Scaling consistency** (all archetypes viable 1-50)
- **Trait flexibility** (customize within archetype)
- **Equipment diversity** (multiple build paths)

### Parody Integration
- **70% Gaming/RPG** - Classic MMO archetypes
- **20% Corporate Satire** - Office personalities
- **10% Meta Humor** - Self-aware character knowledge
