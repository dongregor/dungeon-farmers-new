## Set Bonus Philosophy

**2-Piece**: Minor stat boost (gateway to commitment)
- +5-15% to primary stat
- Light flavor text

**4-Piece**: Significant boost + special effect
- +20-30% to primary stat
- Unique mechanic unlocked
- Build-defining begins

**6-Piece**: Build-defining power
- +35-50% to primary stat(s)
- Ultimate effect (game-changing)
- Visual effects unlocked
- Parody maximized

---

## Design Notes

### Parody Balance
- 70% Gaming/RPG parody (MMO sets, min-maxing, farming)
- 20% Corporate satire (executive gear, office equipment as weapons)
- 10% Pop culture (edgelord aesthetics, anime references)

### Power Scaling
- Sets should feel impactful at 2-piece
- 4-piece should change playstyle
- 6-piece should feel legendary (because it is)
- Mix-matching should be viable (flexibility > rigidity)

### Flavor Text Philosophy
- Self-aware about being a game
- Parodies RPG item descriptions
- Corporate humor where appropriate
- Always light-hearted, never cynical
