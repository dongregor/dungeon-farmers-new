# Achievements Catalog - Complete Achievement List

**Purpose**: Comprehensive catalog of achievements with parody descriptions and actual rewards

**Philosophy**: Achievements track progress, provide goals, and lovingly mock achievement culture

---

## Achievement System Overview

### Categories
1. **Progression** (20) - Level milestones, story completion
2. **Collection** (15) - Monster/hero/equipment gathering
3. **Combat** (12) - Battle performance, efficiency
4. **Social** (8) - Team play, alliances (Phase 3)
5. **Grind** (10) - Repetitive task completion
6. **Parody** (15) - Meta humor, self-aware achievements
7. **Secret** (10) - Hidden, unusual achievements

### Reward Types
- **Cosmetic Titles**: Display on hero cards
- **Gold Bonuses**: One-time gold rewards
- **Equipment**: Unique cosmetic items
- **Bragging Rights**: Literally just bragging rights

## Design Philosophy

### Achievement Purpose
1. **Track Progress**: Milestones visible
2. **Provide Goals**: Long-term objectives
3. **Reward Dedication**: Time investment recognized
4. **Parody Culture**: Mock achievement hunting gently

### Reward Balance
- **Cosmetic Focus**: Titles, decorations (no P2W)
- **Gold Bonuses**: Help progression, not mandatory
- **Special Items**: Unique equipment, not overpowered
- **Bragging Rights**: The real reward

### Parody Integration
- **70% Gaming Parody**: Achievement culture mocked lovingly
- **20% Corporate Satire**: Work achievements paralleled
- **10% Meta Humor**: Self-aware achievement descriptions
