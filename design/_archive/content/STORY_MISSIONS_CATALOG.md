# Story Missions Catalog - Narrative Content

**Purpose**: Comprehensive catalog of story missions with guaranteed rewards and narrative progression

**Philosophy**: Story missions provide structure, guaranteed rewards, and lovingly parody RPG quest tropes

---

## Story Mission System Overview

### Mission Types
- **Main Story**: Progressive narrative (20 missions, levels 1-50)
- **Side Stories**: Optional character narratives (15 missions)
- **Corporate Chronicles**: Office satire storylines (10 missions)
- **Tutorial Arc**: First-time player guidance (5 missions)

### Key Features
- **Guaranteed Rewards**: No RNG, specific hero/item/schematic drops
- **One-Time Completion**: Can't repeat for rewards
- **Story Unlocks**: Progress gates content
- **Parody Elements**: Self-aware humor throughout

## Reward Philosophy

### Guaranteed vs RNG
- **Story Missions**: 100% guaranteed rewards
- **Expeditions**: RNG-based rewards
- **Balance**: Story missions provide baseline progression

### Reward Types
- **Heroes**: Specific archetypes or families guaranteed
- **Equipment**: Named unique items
- **Schematics**: Unlock personal dungeon blueprints
- **Gold**: Substantial amounts for progression
- **Achievements**: Story completion tracking

---

## Design Notes

### Parody Integration
- **70% Gaming/RPG Parody**: Quest tropes, fetch quests, boss battles
- **20% Corporate Satire**: Office politics, meetings, reviews
- **10% Meta Humor**: Self-aware NPCs, fourth wall breaks

### Pacing Philosophy
- **Early (1-10)**: Quick missions, tutorials
- **Mid (11-35)**: Longer, more complex
- **Late (36-50)**: Epic multi-hour challenges

### Narrative Tone
- Self-aware about being a game
- Lovingly mocks corporate culture
- Characters have personality
- Never cynical, always light-hearted
