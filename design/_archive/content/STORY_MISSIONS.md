## ðŸ“– Main Story Arc (Acts 1-9)

### Act 1: Guild Formation (Zones 1-3)

**Mission 0: "First Day at Work"** (Tutorial)
- Unlocks: Basic expedition system
- Rewards: 100 Gold, Tutorial Hero (Theron/Lyra/Magnus)
- Narrative: "Welcome to your new guild! Time to prove yourself."

**Mission 1: "Goblin Trouble"** (After Zone 5)
- Requirements: 2x heroes, Power 200+
- Duration: 15 minutes
- Rewards: Goblin Warrior (guaranteed monster), Goblin Warren (Uncommon schematic), 500 Gold
- Narrative: "Goblin middle management is causing problems. Handle it."
- **Unlocks: Personal Dungeon system**

### Act 2: Growing Influence (Zones 6-10)

**Mission 2: "Dark Forest Mystery"** (After Zone 7)
- Requirements: 1x EXPLORER, 1x SCOUT, Power 350+
- Rewards: Treant Elder (Rare monster), Dark Grove (Rare schematic)
- Narrative: "Something unnatural stirs in the Dark Forest. Investigate."

**Mission 3: "Dragon's Awakening"** (After Zone 10)
- Requirements: 2x RANGED, 1x TANK, 1x HEALER, Power 500+
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Rewards: Dragon Whelp (Rare monster), Dragon's Hoard (Rare schematic), 2,000 Gold
- Narrative: "A young dragon threatens trade routes. Diplomacy or combat?"
- **Unlocks: Alliance system**

### Act 3: Alliance Wars (Zones 11-15)

**Mission 4: "Undead Rising"** (After Zone 12)
- Requirements: 3x heroes with UNDEAD-counter tags
- Rewards: Lich Phylactery (Epic schematic), Death Essence x10
- Narrative: "A necromancer threatens regional stability. Form alliances."

**Mission 5: "Ancient Temple"** (After Zone 15)
- Requirements: Alliance raid participation (or 4x heroes Power 1000+)
- Rewards: Ancient Guardian (Legendary monster), Temple Ruins (Epic schematic)
- Narrative: "Uncover the secrets of the ancients. Cooperate or go alone."
- **Unlocks: Advanced crafting system**

### Act 4: Cosmic Threats (Zones 16-20)

**Mission 6: "Final Confrontation"** (After Zone 20)
- Requirements: 5x heroes Power 2000+, Full Epic+ equipment
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Rewards: Primordial Essence (Mythic material), World's End (Legendary schematic)
- Narrative: "Face the ultimate threat. The guild's legacy is at stake."
- **Unlocks: Endgame content loop**
