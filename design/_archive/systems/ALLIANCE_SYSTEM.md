# Alliance System - Dungeon Farmers

**Social Features Without Pressure - Optional Cooperation**

---

## üéØ Core Philosophy

**Social Benefits. No Mandatory Participation. Solo Play Viable.**

Alliances provide passive bonuses, cooperative raids, and social features WITHOUT making them mandatory for progression.

---

## üè∞ Alliance Structure

### Size & Organization
- **Max 30 members** per alliance
- **Min 5 members** for raid access
- Alliance Leader + Officers (permission management)
- No mandatory participation requirements

### Alliance Levels (1-25)
**Level Up Through:**
- Member contributions (gold, materials)
- Alliance raid completions
- Weekly quest completion

**Level Benefits:**
- Higher passive bonuses
- Access to higher-tier raids
- Better alliance shop offerings

---

## üí∞ Passive Bonuses (Always Active)

### Alliance-Wide Buffs
**Scales with alliance level:**

- **+5-15% Gold** (Level 1 ‚Üí Level 25)
- **+3-10% Experience** (Level 1 ‚Üí Level 25)
- **+1-5% Rare Drop Rate** (Level 10 ‚Üí Level 25)

**Design Rationale**: Passive bonuses benefit ALL members, including casuals who don't raid.

---

## üêâ Alliance Raids

### Raid Participation
- **3-5 players** (NOT entire alliance)
- Flexible scheduling (no mandatory times)
- Real-time coordination via chat
- Weekly resets per raid tier

### Raid Tiers
**Beginner Raids**: Alliance Level 5+, Uncommon/Rare rewards
**Intermediate Raids**: Alliance Level 10+, Rare/Epic rewards
**Advanced Raids**: Alliance Level 15+, Epic/Legendary rewards
**Elite Raids**: Alliance Level 20+, Legendary/Mythic rewards, rare monsters

---

## üí¨ Social Features

### Real-Time Chat
- Alliance-wide chat channel
- Raid party chat during expeditions
- Friend system for cross-alliance communication

### Alliance Shop
- Weekly rotating Epic/Legendary equipment
- Purchase with Alliance Points (earned from raids)
- Schematics, materials, cosmetics available

### Future Features
- Shared dungeon library (rent schematics from allies)
- Alliance wars (territory control, optional PvP)
- Voice chat for raids (Discord integration)

---

## üéØ Design Principles Applied

### No Mandatory Participation
- Solo players can progress without alliance
- Alliance bonuses enhance but don't gatekeep
- Casual members welcome (passive bonuses benefit everyone)

### Flexible Raid Scheduling
- 3-5 players can organize at ANY time
- No "everyone must be online at 8pm" requirements
- Weekly resets allow flexible completion

### Social Without Pressure
- Chat is opt-in
- Raids are optional
- No minimum contribution requirements
- No alliance "taxes" or mandatory donations

---

**The Alliance System provides meaningful social features and cooperative gameplay while respecting that many players prefer solo or casual play. All benefits are enhancements, not requirements.**
